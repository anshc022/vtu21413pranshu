About the Event

The event offers a chance to solve real-world problems using logic. Participants engage with diverse case studies from various industries, crafting innovative solutions through critical thinking. It's a hands-on opportunity to navigate complexities, collaborate with peers, and gain recognition for problem-solving skills. Don't miss out—join us for a journey of learning and discovery!

Take advantage of this chance to showcase your skills and network with peers in the engineering community.

Prizes will be awarded to the top five pitches.

Event Information

The event will take place on Mar 19th, 2024.

Venue: Engineering Hive (4303).

The contest will start at 1:30 PM.

Half day OD will be provided.

Limited Slots: Only the first 15 teams can register.

Secure your spot early to avoid missing out on this exciting opportunity!

Rules

  • Presentation Format: Bring your PowerPoint (PPT) presentation.
  • Team Size: Keep it small – 3 members maximum per team
  • Punctuality: Be on time for your presentation slot
  • Content: Cover key points like problem-solving and market analysis
  • Time: Stick to the time limit.
  • Professionalism: Stay professional during your presentation.
  • Q&A: Be ready for questions from the judges.
  • Originality: Your idea must be original.
  • Materials: Bring any extra stuff you need.
